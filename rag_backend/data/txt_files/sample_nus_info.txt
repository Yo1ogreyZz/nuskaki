# NUS Campus Information Sample

National University of Singapore (NUS) is Singapore's flagship university.

## Central Library

The Central Library is located at 12 Kent Ridge Crescent, Singapore 119275.

Opening Hours:
- Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM
- Saturday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- Sunday: Closed

Services:
- Book borrowing and returns
- Study spaces
- Computer workstations
- Printing services
- Group discussion rooms

## University Town (UTown)

UTown is a vibrant residential and educational complex.

Facilities:
- Residential Colleges
- Teaching facilities
- Sports facilities including swimming pool and gym
- Food courts and cafes
- Stephen Riady Centre

Location: University Town, NUS Campus

## Student Services

NUS provides comprehensive student services:

1. Student Affairs Office
   - Located at University Hall
   - Handles student welfare and activities
   - Office hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Weekdays)

2. Career Centre
   - Provides career guidance and counseling
   - Organizes career fairs and workshops
   - Located at MD5, Level 2

3. Health Services
   - University Health Centre at Kent Ridge
   - Open daily for medical consultations
   - Emergency services available 24/7

## Getting Around Campus

NUS provides free internal shuttle bus services:

- Bus A1, A2: Main campus loop
- Bus D1, D2: Between Kent Ridge and UTown
- Bus K: Kent Ridge to nearby MRT stations

Operating hours: 7:00 AM - 11:00 PM daily

## Dining Options

The campus has numerous dining options:

- Techno Edge Canteen
- The Deck (near University Hall)
- Frontier Canteen
- Engineering Canteen
- Science Canteen
- UTown Food Court

Most canteens are open from 7:30 AM to 8:00 PM on weekdays.
